A [planar stack](Planar Stack) is a collection of tightly grouped [planes](Planes), usually holding strong connections to each other. 

A [planar stack](Planar Stack) consists of a series of [planes](Planes), stacked on top of each other, all within a bubble of energy. While the laws of nature, magic, and physics differ from one stack to the next, their overarching structure are generally the same. As one travels from the cosmos to a [planar stack](Planar Stack), to a plane, the number of dimensions steadily decrease.
