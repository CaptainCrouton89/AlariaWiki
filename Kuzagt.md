## Overview

The Kuzagt, also known as Bone Elves, are tall, spindly elves known for their enslavement of humans. 

## Physical Appearance

Kuzagt have high cheekbones with strange, bony protrusions coming from them. Their features are more angular than other elves, and they have more slanted eyes.

## Cultural Overview

The Kuzagt place little value on human lives—seeing them as invaders and poachers on the great beauty that is Alaria. They see nothing wrong with using them to further their own purposes. However, many Kuzagt have taken this too far, and have lost sight of the original, deeper purpose.
