## Overview

Reside in [Eros](Eros).

## Physical Appearance

Literal [ghosts](Ghosts), trapped within the Eros [forests](Forests). They are descendents of [Caerine](Caerine) [elves](Elves), but sought to fly too close to the sun—reaching for immortality and oneness with the [forest](Forests). Instead, they eternally bound their souls to the forest, and although their souls will never die so long as the forest lives, their souls are trapped their.
