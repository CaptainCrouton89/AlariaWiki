The eastern-most continent of [Alaria](Alaria). 

[Ve](Ve) contains the following states:

- Edari
- [Shyona](Shyona)
- [Sestros](Sestros)
- [Thorum](Thorum)
- [Avalon](Avalon)
- [Dern](Dern)
- [Chimea](Chimea)

In the far east, [Krell](Krell) hives dot the [jungle](Jungles), and in the far south, [dinosaurs](Dinosaurs) roam.
